Last Updated: February 10, 2026

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Analyze website traffic and visitor behavior
  • Improve landing page performance
  • Support marketing and ad campaigns
  • Remember user preferences

Types of Cookies Used

Essential Cookies

Required for website functionality.

Analytics Cookies

Used to track visitor interactions and improve UX.

Marketing Cookies

Used for ad performance tracking when running campaigns.

Third-Party Cookies

Ad platforms and analytics providers may place cookies when users interact with our ads or website.

User Control

Users can disable cookies through browser settings. Some website features may not function properly if disabled.

We may update this Cookie Policy as marketing and analytics tools evolve.
