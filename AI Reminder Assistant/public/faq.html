Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about YAAD AI

General

YAAD AI is an AI-powered reminder assistant that works directly in WhatsApp and Telegram. You can set reminders using natural language, voice notes, and even in Hindi or Hinglish. No app downloads or signups required!

Simply click on the "Start on WhatsApp" or "Try on Telegram" button on our homepage. Send a message like "Remind me to call mom tomorrow at 5pm" and you're all set! The bot will confirm your reminder and notify you at the right time.

No! YAAD AI works entirely within WhatsApp and Telegram. You just need to have one of these messaging apps installed, which you probably already do.

Yes! YAAD AI is currently 100% free with unlimited reminders. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.

Features

Absolutely! Just send a voice note saying something like "Remind me to buy groceries tomorrow at 6pm" and YAAD AI will understand and create your reminder.

Yes! You can set reminders in English, Hindi, or Hinglish. For example: "Kal subah 7 baje exercise karne ki yaad dilana" or "Remind me kal 3 baje meeting hai" both work perfectly.

Yes! You can set daily, weekly, monthly, or custom recurring reminders. Just say "Remind me to take medicine every day at 9am" or "Meeting every Monday at 3pm" and YAAD AI handles the rest.

Privacy & Security

Yes! All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We use WhatsApp-grade security and never share your personal information with third parties. Your reminders are private and only accessible to you.

We only store the reminder information you explicitly create. We don't store your entire chat history, only the reminders you've set and their details (time, message, recurrence).

Technical Support

The reminder message will stay in your chat history, so you can always see it when you check your messages. For recurring reminders, the next occurrence will still trigger as scheduled.

First, check your internet connection. If that's fine, try sending a simple message like "Hi" to restart the conversation. If issues persist, contact us through the support channels on our homepage.
