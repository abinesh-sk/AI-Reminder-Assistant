Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we handle your data.

Last Updated: February 10, 2026

Yaad AI ("we", "our", "us") operates as an AI-powered reminder and personal assistant service accessible via chat-based platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect user data.

Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Phone number or messaging platform ID
  • Reminder content and scheduling data
  • Voice notes (processed for transcription only)
  • Usage logs such as timestamps and activity events
  • Language preferences

We do not collect passwords, banking details, or personal device data.

How We Use Data

Data is used strictly to:

  • Schedule and deliver reminders
  • Maintain reminder history
  • Improve assistant responses
  • Monitor system performance

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data.

Data Storage

  • Reminder data is stored securely in encrypted databases
  • Retention period: up to 15-30 days reminder history
  • Completed reminders may be auto-deleted after retention window

Voice Data Handling

  • Voice notes are converted to text via speech-to-text APIs
  • Used only for reminder creation
  • Not permanently stored unless required for logs

Third-Party Services

We may use secure providers for:

  • Messaging APIs
  • Hosting infrastructure
  • AI text processing

All partners follow industry security standards.

User Rights

Users may request:

  • Reminder data deletion
  • Account unlinking
  • History removal

Requests can be made via support email.

Security

We implement encrypted API connections, credential-restricted backend access, and abuse monitoring.

Policy Updates

This policy may evolve as features expand. Continued use implies acceptance.
